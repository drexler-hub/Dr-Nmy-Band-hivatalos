The great Greek storyteller,
who lived for thousands of years,
is best known for his animal histories.
Out of the hundreds of surviving stories,
we have highlighted the ones we like best,
because what they have to say is still very
relevant today for children and adults alike.
Songs were made from the stories in Hungarian.
